Schoology Helper

Castaneda Grader Scraper

Install the bookmarklet once, use it from the Schoology submissions page, then upload the downloaded CSV into your grader setup screen.

Card 1

Install Bookmarklet

Turn on your bookmarks bar first with Cmd+Shift+B on Mac or Ctrl+Shift+B on Windows.

📄 Scrape Iran Submissions

Drag that button to your bookmarks bar, or copy the code below and save it as a bookmark manually.

Card 2

Scrape

Go to Schoology, open the Iran Summative submissions page, scroll all the way to the bottom, then click the bookmarklet.

The browser downloads schoology_submissions_iran.csv. Student names need to match the roster closely for the upload matcher to succeed.

Card 3

Upload to Grader

Open your teacher grader, go through Setup Step 2, and upload the downloaded CSV.

Open Castaneda Grader

The upload confirmation modal will show matched and unmatched names before anything writes to the shared sheet.
